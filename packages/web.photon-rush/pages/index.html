Solar Genesis

A narrative driven game that combines role-playing, factory building and incremental gameplay.

The ISV Enceladus is trapped between the stars. Take command, repair the damaged starship and uncover the mystery that threatens 10,000 lives.

In Solar Genesis you will:

  • Make friends with artificial intelligences who will help you with repairs.
  • Make enemies with artificial intelligences who will do anything to stop you.
  • Build a factory to repair Enceladus.
  • Build and upgrade drones to fight and repair.
  • Hack the system.
  • Automate EVERYTHING with a built-in visual programming language or JavaScript.

Coming soon to Steam, Discord, GitHub and your computer.
